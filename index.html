Background

Naturally, this lead me into the Retail field. ::laughter:: I worked my way up to the management level internally where I've worked for the better part of the last ten years. This path taught me not only to work as a part of a team but how to best get the most out of myself and the people around me.

In late 2020, I decided to try something completely new: learn how to code. I enrolled in Actualize Coding Bootcamp and participated in the class, finishing the course in January of 2021. The entire process was a blast and I'm incredibly glad I did it.

overBoard

For my final project at Actualize, I created my app called overBoard. It represents a culmination of my efforts at the bootcamp and I'm very proud of it.

overBoard is a place for board game enthusiasts to create a profile, browse the game database of over 100 games and digitally recreate their at-home game collection. They can then browse other member's collections and create meetups with those users to play their favorite games.
