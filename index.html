Background

One thing you should know about me: I love language! In college, I studied abroad in Granada, Spain and learned so much about the Spanish language and culture. Did you know that when you buy a beer in Granada, you get FREE tapas? Amazing.

Naturally, this lead me into the Retail field. I worked my way up to the management level internally where I've worked for the better part of the last ten years. This job taught me not only to work as a part of a team but how to best get the most out of my team.

Why Coding?

I believe my love of language has lead me into the coding world. I always love the feeling of working through a problem and then finally getting it to work! I've already had a number of these moments in my early days as a developer, and I strive for more.

Also, I just love working with computers.
